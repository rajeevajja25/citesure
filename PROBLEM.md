# Problem Statement

## The Problem
Generative AI and online content lack a reliable mechanism to ensure that statements are backed by verifiable, trustworthy evidence.

## Impact
- Misinformation spreads unchecked
- Weak research integrity in academic work  
- Loss of trust in legal and policy documents
- AI hallucinations present false information as fact

## Our Solution
CiteGuard: An evidence-first citation verification system that:
1. Retrieves evidence BEFORE generating conclusions
2. Uses only trusted sources (PubMed, academic databases, government data)
3. Provides highlight-anchored citations with exact quotes
4. Shows confidence scores with transparent reasoning
